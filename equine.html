Universal Block

Equine lick block is formulated to provide the nutrients required by horses as recommended by National Research Council (NRC) guidelines.

Salt is the most important mineral for horses with calcium and phosphorus coming in a close second.

Vitamin A is added to help improve the stability of cell membranes and maintain the integrity of the mucosal barrier.

Vitamin E plays biological functions such as anti-oxidation, maintaining fertility and regulating the immune system, and protects the cell membrane from damage by free radicals.

Applicable stage: all stages of horses

Ingredients:

Salt(NaCl).......................................................96%

Iron(Fe).......................................................400ppm

Magnesium(Mg)...............................................800ppm

Copper(Cu)...................................................150ppm

Zinc(Zn)........................................................1200ppm

Selenium(Se)......................................................33ppm

Calcium(Ca).....................................................2500ppm

Total Phosphorus(TP).........................................2000ppm

Vitamin A....................................................4100 IU/Ib.

IU/Ib. Vitamin E...............................................450IU/Ib.
