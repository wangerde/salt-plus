Fertility Block

Improve the survival rate and newborn weight of calves

Due to the rapid growth of the fetus and the formation of colostrum in late pregnancy, cows are prone to lack VE and Se, resulting in an increase in their morbidity.

Supplementing VE and Se to pregnant cows can improve their reproductive performance and breast health and reduce the morbidity and mortality of calves.

Applicable stage: 12 to 24-month-old cows and lactation period

Ingredients:

Salt(NaCl)...............................................................97%

Iron(Fe)..............................................................500ppm

Cobalt(Co)............................................................40ppm

Selenium(Se)...........................................................30ppm

Copper(Cu)..........................................................150ppm

Zinc(Zn)....................................................................800ppm

Magnesium(Mg)...........................................800ppm

Manganese(Mn).......................................................600ppm

Vitamin A.......................................................3600 IU/Ib.

Vitamin E..........................................................450 IU/Ib.
